About

"Nesha Parwez Helping Human Foundation" is dedicated to improving the lives of disadvantaged children by providing access to education, basic healthcare, and essential resources. We partner with local volunteers and donors to run school supply drives, free health camps, mentorship programs, and community awareness activities. Our approach focuses on transparency, accountability, and long-term community empowerment.

Mission

  • Provide quality education resources to children from low-income families
  • Offer basic health and nutrition support
  • Run local skill-building and awareness programs

Vision

Every child deserves an opportunity. We aim to build a stronger community by investing in education, health, and sustainable support systems.
